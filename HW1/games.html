Where Are the Games?

My Work

I don't have the bulk of my tabletop work archived online (oops!), but a pretty comprehensive collection of my digital work can be found on my itch.io page. I did the bulk of the coding for everything uploaded there, though I do collaborate with my friends on projects.

We use GML (think Javascript or a stripped down C, but significantly more forgiving) so that my friends who don't have a formal CS education don't get too bogged down on the specifics of the language and can jump right into logic and algorithms.
