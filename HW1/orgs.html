Where to Catch Me

Corvallis Game Devs

I help run this one! The Corvallis Game Devs are a smallish group of devs that operate out of Corvallis and surrounding areas.

We meet twice monthly along with a game jam every couple of months. Come stop by some time! The schedule's on the website.

PIGSquad

The Portland Indie Game Squad (known colloquially as PIGSquad) is a relatively massive cooperative based in Portland.

They often throw showcase events, where devs can come and show what they've made to large crowds.
